June 2024 — Present
Senior UX/UI Designer
Tietoevry (UHC)

Senior UX Designer collaborating with a large U.S. healthcare provider to improve onboarding experiences for medical professionals in a regulated environment. I led discovery — stakeholder interviews, documentation analysis, and group usability testing — and translated insights into clear, efficient onboarding flows. I owned the full UX process (discovery, flow definition, information architecture, wireframing, prototyping, and usability testing) for a mobile app built from scratch, and redesigned the design system to support a new product experience while staying cohesive with the existing brand — ensuring scalability and consistency across touchpoints. Day to day, I simplify complex workflows, reduce friction, and improve usability across desktop and responsive interfaces, working iteratively with cross-functional teams (product, engineering, stakeholders) to balance user needs, business goals, and healthcare constraints.

May 2022 — Present
Freelance — Senior UX/UI Designer
Beett · Third Term · Miller Zell · DMO

I've worked across a wide range of digital products — from corporate training experiences and communication bots to user flows and visual systems that improve how people interact with technology. I've contributed to health-related products, including a medical app and a migraine-treatment solution for large pharmaceutical companies, as well as consumer wellness branding. Most recently I worked on a content-driven mobile app for marketing professionals — sharing articles, videos, and location-based recommendations through an interactive map, helping teams document experiences, showcase atmosphere and design details, and inspire others through real-world insights.

May 2022 — June 2024
UX/UI Designer
Galicia Bank

UX/UI Designer within the Payments stream of a large-scale banking app, designing high-impact, transaction-critical experiences. I analyzed user behavior with tools like Hotjar, authored UX briefs, mapped user flows, and designed from wireframes to high-fidelity interactive prototypes. I led the UX design of a new banking product end-to-end — from research and proto-persona definition to information architecture, UI design, and usability testing — collaborating closely with product and engineering to ensure usability, consistency, and alignment with business and regulatory requirements.

February 2021 — January 2024
Senior Product Designer
Carestino

Responsible for adapting and redesigning products originally manufactured in China for multiple Latin American markets. I led product modifications to meet local regulatory requirements and cultural expectations across countries, ensuring compliance, usability, and market acceptance. My work spanned physical and digital touchpoints — redesigning product features, updating technical specifications, and adapting supporting assets like websites and user manuals. I collaborated with cross-functional teams so each market version reflected local language nuances, cultural context, and usage patterns, recognizing that even shared languages require distinct design considerations to earn adoption and trust.
